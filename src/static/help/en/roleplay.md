# 🎭 Roleplay & Rules

Welcome to **Alex Tavern**, a multi-agent roleplay engine! Here is how the world works:

## Core Roles
- **Narrator**: Governs physical facts, immediate consequences, and decides who reacts next.
- **Characters (NPCs)**: Act and express their thoughts or speech in first person, within their own private context.
- **Player (You)**: Controls a single character. You dictate their speech, action, and private thoughts.

## Immersion & Agency
To maintain deep immersion, the AI models do not know a "Player" exists. Everything is translated to your character's name before reaching the LLMs.
