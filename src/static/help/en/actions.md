# ⚙️ Action Menu

Discover advanced action controls next to the Send button.

## How to Open
- **On Desktop**: Hover your mouse cursor over the **Send** button area.
- **On Mobile**: **Press and hold (long-press)** the **Send** button for 600ms.

## Available Actions
- 🎲 **Force Speaker**: Dropdown select to force a specific speaker (Narrator, NPCs, or Automatic).
- ⏭ **Skip Turn**: Pass your turn without input, letting the environment or NPCs act.
- 💡 **Suggestion**: Request 3 plausible in-character suggestions from the Narrator.
- 📜 **Narrator Hint**: Guide the Narrator by injecting an upcoming environmental event.
- ↩ **Undo**: Revert the last turn step.
- 🗜️ **Compact**: Compress older history into notes and summaries.
