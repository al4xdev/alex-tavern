# ⚡ AI Engine & Settings

Configure the engine to match your model's capabilities.

## Key Configurations
- **Context Max**: The maximum context window size allowed. For DeepSeek, this can be extremely large (up to 1M tokens).
- **Max Tokens**: Token caps for Narrator, Character responses, and the Summarizer.
- **Thinking**: Enable or disable reasoning capabilities for models that support them.

## Long stories

**Automatic compaction** can summarize older events before a long adventure fills the model's
reading space. Its slider controls timing: move left to summarize earlier with more free space, or
right to summarize less often and use more of the available space. The `?` beside the control opens
the complete plain-language guide.
